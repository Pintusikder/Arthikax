1. Introduction

Welcome to ArthikaX ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your business and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how ArthikaX handles your data.

ArthikaX is designed as a completely offline application. This means that all your data remains securely stored on your own device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third-party services.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

ArthikaX does NOT collect, transmit, or store any of the following information on external servers:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Business transaction data (entries, parties, amounts, etc.)
  • Financial records or account balances
  • Device location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Cookies or tracking identifiers
  • IP addresses or network information

3. How Your Data is Stored

3.1 Local Storage Only

All data you enter into ArthikaX is stored locally on your device using:

  • IndexedDB: For structured business data (transactions, parties, collections)
  • localStorage: For app settings and preferences

3.2 Data Security

Your data is protected through:

  • AES-256 Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption
  • PIN Lock: Optional PIN protection to prevent unauthorized access
  • No Cloud Sync: Data never leaves your device
  • No External Dependencies: App works 100% offline without internet connection

4. Third-Party Services

ArthikaX does NOT use any third-party services, analytics tools, or tracking mechanisms. We do not integrate with:

  • Google Analytics or any analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Cloud storage services
  • Payment processors
  • Any other external services that could access your data

5. Data Backup and Export

Since your data is stored locally, YOU are responsible for backing up your data. ArthikaX provides:

  • PDF Export: Export reports and invoices as PDF files
  • Local Backups: Data remains in your browser's local storage

Important: Clearing your browser data, uninstalling the app, or resetting your device will permanently delete all ArthikaX data. We CANNOT recover your data as it is never stored on our servers.

6. Permissions

ArthikaX may request the following device permissions:

  • Storage: To save PDF exports to your device
  • Camera (future feature): To scan bills or receipts (optional, if implemented)

These permissions are used solely for the stated features and never for data collection.

7. Children's Privacy

ArthikaX is designed for business use and is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information to us, please note that we do not collect any data.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

8.1 How Long We Keep Your Data

We do NOT keep your data because we never receive it. All data exists only on your device.

8.2 How to Delete Your Data

You can delete your data at any time by:

  1. Using the "Clear All Data" feature in the app settings

  3. Clearing your browser's local storage for the ArthikaX domain

Warning: Data deletion is permanent and cannot be recovered.
