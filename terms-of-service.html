2. Description of Service

ArthikaX is a free, offline-first Progressive Web Application (PWA) designed to help small businesses and individuals:

  • Track credit (udhar) and debit transactions
  • Maintain digital party ledgers (khata book)
  • Record daily cash collections
  • Generate business reports and invoices
  • Manage multiple business profiles
  • Calculate denomination totals for cash counting

The App is designed to work 100% offline with all data stored locally on your device.
