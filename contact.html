Bug Reporting Guidelines

If you've encountered a bug or technical issue, please include:

  1. Description: Clear description of the problem
  2. Steps to Reproduce: How to recreate the issue
  3. Expected Behavior: What should have happened
  4. Actual Behavior: What actually happened
  5. Device/Browser: Your device type, browser name and version
  6. Screenshots: If applicable, attach screenshots

Frequently Asked Questions

‚ùì How do I back up my data?
ArthikaX stores all data locally on your device. To back up, use the PDF export feature to save reports. For manual backup, your browser's data export tools can be used, but we DO NOT provide cloud backup to maintain privacy.
‚ùì I forgot my PIN. Can you recover it?
No, we CANNOT recover your PIN as it's stored encrypted on your device. For security reasons, if you forget your PIN, you'll need to reset the app (which will delete all data). We recommend noting your PIN somewhere secure.
‚ùì Does ArthikaX work on iOS/iPhone?
Yes! ArthikaX is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works on all devices with modern browsers, including iOS/iPhone. Use Safari to access the app and add it to your home screen for app-like experience.
‚ùì Can I sync data across multiple devices?
Currently, ArthikaX does NOT support cloud sync to maintain complete privacy. All data stays on your device. You can export data as PDF from one device and manually import/re-enter on another device.
‚ùì Is my business data safe?
Absolutely! Your data never leaves your device. We use AES-256 encryption for sensitive data and offer PIN lock protection. Since we never see or store your data on servers, your privacy is 100% guaranteed.
‚ùì How can I contribute to ArthikaX?
ArthikaX is open source! You can contribute by reporting bugs, suggesting features, improving documentation. We welcome community contributions!
‚ùì Do you provide professional support or customization?
ArthikaX is a free, community-driven project. While we provide general support via email and GitHub, we do not currently offer paid professional support or custom development. However, since it's open source, you're free to fork and customize it yourself!
